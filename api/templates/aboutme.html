Our Goals

Pitman Parking: Your guide to quick parking! Pitman Parking was built to help drivers find a parking spot seamlessly. With Pitman Parking, drivers just enter, view the available spots, and park. Pitman Parking lets you know which floor is filled, and where is the nearest spot relative to your position. No need for circling around, U-turns, nor wasting time. This browser-friendly website is delivered at no cost and was built for quick and easy access. Upon arrival to your destination garage, simply scan the Pitman Parking QR code at the entrance with your mobile device. Then, select which browser to open the website. Pitman Parking displays a grid view of all spots, separated per floor based on your location, where each spot is color-coded based on occupancy for readability purposes: red for Occupied, and green for Free. Once you find the spot you prefer, drive towards it and you're done!
